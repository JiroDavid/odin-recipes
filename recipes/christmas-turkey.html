Christmas Roasted Turkey

Head back to our Home Page

Serve a traditional roast turkey for Christmas without the fuss.

Use the sticky glaze you'd get on a ham to add flavour and golden colour to a turkey crown

Here are the ingredients:

  • 50g butter
  • 2kg turkey
  • 1 tsp Chinese five spice

    • For the glaze:

  • 4 tsbp honey
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar

Method:

  1. Heat oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 5.
  2. Smear the butter all over the turkey crown and season all over with salt and half the five spice.
  3. Put in a roasting tin, skin-side up, and roast for 30 mins.
  4. While the crown is roasting, mix all the glaze ingredients in a bowl with the rest of the five spice.
  5. Remove the crown from the oven and brush the skin generously with half the glaze.
  6. Continue to roast for another hour glazing twice more, until cooked all the way through.
  7. Leave to rest for atleast 20 minutes before carving. Save the juices from the tin to make gravy.
